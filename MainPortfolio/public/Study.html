Happiness Project

Objective

This project is a skill track application aimed to increase self-esteem, enhance self development and daily living with a sense of accomplishing the goals the user has made for themselves. By tracking the skills the user wants to accomplish, the user will be able to see the progression they have made over a period of time as well as have a timeline to complete that certain skill.

Purpose of Application

  • Asset people to self-evaluate themselves.
  • Specify daily To-Do list and interactively recognize their overall work.
  • Enable people to be consistent.
  • Help people to actively(consciousnessly) choose their actions.
  • Motivate people to improve themselves by using gamification concept.
  • Prioritize tasks to help them to identify which task is more value.

Needs of Application

  • Giving the individual a space to track their progress without having to compare themselves to others.
  • Provide people a strctured task list for those who have a hard time concentrating on their work/tasks.
  • Give people the push who have plans, but do not have strong desire to put it in action.
  • Poeple who are easily distrcted with social media.
  • Guid people who have a problem with distinguishing what is valuable within their life.

Project outcomes

  • Self-Evaluation - Estimate their current limitation and define what they are able to do at the current time.
  • Motivation - Encourage themselves to improvee their lifestyle by accomplishing the goals set.
  • Consistency - Be consistent on their daily goals.
  • Priority - Identify what goas are urgent and important in their life.
  • Self-Esteem - Increase self-esteem by achieving and accomplishing goals.
